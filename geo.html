Having two Geo characters in the group results in the Elemental Resonance:

Enduring Rock: Increases shield strength by 15%. Additionally, characters protected by a shield will have the following special characteristics: DMG dealt increased by 15%, dealing DMG to enemies will decrease their Geo RES by 20% for 15s.
Crystallize: Absorbs the element and drops a crystal of that same element. The crystal will give an elemental shield. The shield absorbs all types of damage, but it protects more efficiently against the shield's element.

Similar to Anemo, Geo is ephemeral and will either react immediately with preapplied elements or disappear instantly after application if no other elements are applied when applied to an enemy.
Another exception exists: Zhongli's Elemental Burst, Planet Befall, is capable of applying a persistent Geo status effect for the duration of his Petrification
