The Map is an interactive representation of the world. It can be accessed by pressing the Minimap in the top left of the screen, opening from Paimon Menu > Map, or pressing the "M" key on PC. Teleport Waypoints, Statues of the Seven, and Domains can be teleported to by selecting them on the Map and pressing "Teleport".

Pressing the "Domains only" toggle at the bottom left of the screen shows the Domain names and their category.

Subareas are labeled on the Map. The current area and its Exploration Progress is labeled at the bottom right of the Map. When fully zoomed out, Domains and areas (with their Exploration Progress percentage) are labeled on the Map.

Other information available on the Map Menu include Notifications, Spiral Abyss progress, and Original Resin count. Different Maps can be chosen by pressing the icon at the bottom right of the screen. These currently include Teyvat and Serenitea Pot. Golden Apple Archipelago was also listed during Version 1.6.

While the player can move the Map around and view inaccessible areas, the player cannot venture into Out of Bounds areas.
Upon beginning the game, the Map is initially limited to about half of Starfell Valley plus, roughly, Windrise, Falcon Coast, and Springvale.
Upon completion of The Outlander Who Caught the Wind (which quest?), the rest of the map becomes available.
Unlocking a Statue of The Seven lights up that Statue's area on the Map.
