Genshin Impact follows a similar free-to-play model as many "gacha" games. This means that while you start the game with a character known as the Traveler, and are able to unlock a few others by playing through the main story - the bulk of characters can only be unlocked by "pulling" from a pool of possible weapons and characters by using currency.
